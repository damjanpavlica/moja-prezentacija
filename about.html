About

Welcome to a world filled with the magic of friendship! The Mane 6 are a group of pony friends who always stick together on adventures through the town of Ponyville, the world of Equestria, and beyond. Inspired by the animated television series, My Little Pony Friendship is Magic, the Meet the Mane 6 Ponies Collection includes these favorite pony figures: Twilight Sparkle, Pinkie Pie, Rainbow Dash, Fluttershy, Rarity, and Applejack. They are sculpted with character-inspired expressions and signature poses. This collection is a great introduction for My Little Pony beginners to grow new friendships within a beloved brand.
